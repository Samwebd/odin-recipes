Cacio e Pepe Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Cup Grated Parmesan
  • 1/3 Cup Grated Pecorino Romano
  • 2 Tbs Butter
  • 1 Tbs Coarsely Ground
    Black Pepper
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Bring pot of salted water to boil and cook pasta untill almost done.

  2. Roast pepper in a large skillet with butter untill fragant, about 1 minute.

  3. Add pasta and a ladle of pasta water to skillet to finish cooking the pasta.

  4. When the water has evaporated from the skillet, melt in the grated cheese and serve.
