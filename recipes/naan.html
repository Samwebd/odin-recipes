Naan Recipe

Ingredients

  • Water
  • 2 Tsp Active Dry Yeast
  • 1/2 Tsp Sugar
  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Tbs Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast, sugar, and 1/2 cup of flour in a large bowl. Let sit uncovered for 15 minutes untill it bubbles.

  2. After the mixture bubbles, add salt, olive oil, and 2 cups of flour. Stir until mixture forms a shaggy mass. Dust with flour and knead in the bowl to fully incorporate.

  3. Transfer the dough to a dusted surface and knead untill smooth. Let it rest for 10 minutes and then knead for a few more minutes, adding flour as nesessary. Then cover and let rise for 1 hour.

  4. After 1 hour, deflate the dough and divide it into 8 balls. Roll out each ball into a circle 8-9" wide and 1/4" thick.

  5. Lay rolled out dough one at a time in a skillet with olive oil on medium-high heat. Cook for 30 seconds on on side untill bubbles start to form. Flip and cook the other side for 1-2 minutes untill toasted spots appear. Flip again and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
    Serve hot.
